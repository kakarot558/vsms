CSV Format Guide

Your CSV must include these columns (header row required):

product_name, barcode, category, brand, description, price, cost_price, stock, unit, low_stock_threshold, requires_prescription
  • product_name and price are required. All other columns are optional.
  • If a barcode already exists, the product will be updated (not duplicated).
  • requires_prescription: use 1 for yes, 0 for no.
  • Leave barcode blank to auto-generate one.
